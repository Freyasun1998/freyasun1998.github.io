Introduction The Introduction of Data Set and My Topic Author: js4958


The dataset is the outcome of a web-crawling exercise aimed at identifying employment opportunities that could potentially interest DSAN students.
There are roughly 85 searches, each yielding up to 10 job postings, for a total of around 850 jobs, which are currently active online, as of 04/14/2023.
The postings were obtained using the following search query terms: "data scientist","data analyst","neural networks","big data and cloud computing","machine learning", 'reinforcement learning','deep learning', "time series","block chain","natural language processing".


In this project, I analyzed four questions that many DSAN students concerned about when they plan for their career. Students are concerned about the difference of data analysts and data scientists from the aspect of skills required and years of experience required.
The choice of elective courses is also very essential for DSAN students when planning for their career. Students interested in the field of deep learning tend to select ANLY-590 in their fall semester and ANLY-591 in their fall semester. So I chose this field to analyze job description of related positions.
For some Gorgetown University students who are unwilling to relocate to other states, they only want to focus on companies in DC area. Besides DC area, California is also one of the best states in the US for tech jobs. Download Dataset

Part 1. Data Analyst vs Data Scientist

Similarity vs Difference


I drew two wordclouds based on the job description of data analyst and data scientist respectively to identify the similarity and difference of these two positions.
Similarity: These two positions both require candidates to have a business sense and analytic skills to provide business solutions.
Difference: Data scientist positions require candidates to have machine learning knowledge and be be able to develope models. Data analyst positions require a stronger analysis ability, and report writing ability to support management.

Years of Experience Required


This plot shows the minimum years of experience required in job description of data analyst and data scientist. Generally, both data analysts and data scientists are expected to have at least 2-5 years of experience. The maximum years of experience required for a data analyst is 8 years, while the maimum years of experience required for a data scientist can be up to 13 years. Part 1 Code
